Bringing application services and users together-the electronic mail 	example
More and more people are using electronic mail systems. However, virtually 	no efforts have been made to examine in how far services provided 	by these systems really meet the actual user requirements. As an 	initial step, a rough analysis of user demands is given. This is 	followed by a brief introduction to CCITT's X.400/X.500 recommendations, 	which will form the basis of all future multivendor mail systems. 	Services defined in these recommendations are matched against the 	idenitfied requirements. Finally, some useful enhancements of X.400/X.500 	are proposed
