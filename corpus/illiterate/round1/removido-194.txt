Active capture and folk computing [multimedia computing]
The domains of folk computing applications touch on areas of interest 	to people around the world but are of pressing need to those in the 	developing world who often lack access to basic services and rights: 	especially health care, education, nutrition, and protection of human 	rights. In this paper, we describe how a new paradigm for media capture, 	called active capture, and toolkit support for creating applications 	of this type, work toward supporting the development of multimedia 	applications and interfaces for folk computing
