Constructivism learning theory for programming through an e-learning
Abstract: Evolutions are happening in teaching and learning process with the used of e-learning. It was an innovative technology that was implemented in many different fields regarding to its benefits. This paper presents a constructivism learning theory for programming through an e-learning with the assumption that constructivism is an important branch of cognitivism. This learning theory that proposed in learning programming hopefully will help to improve level of motivation among novice.
