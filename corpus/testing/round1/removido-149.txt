Business Software Testing Model Design: A Theoretical Framework
Abstract  The article analyses the theoretical aspects relevant to designing business software testing models, describing  the evolution of software, and argumenting the case in favour of the need for continuous improvement of software  testing. The results of prominent studies in the domain of software testing, featuring as the starting point  for business software testing model design are analysed. The final part of the article defines a research model  which will serve as a basis for research whose outcome will define the business software testing model design.
