Cross Browser Incompatibility: Reasons and Solutions
ABSTRACT  A good Web site is more than just something to look at, it is functional interactive and flawless. As  technologies are becoming smart so we need to be smarter enough to utilize them. With the rapid evolution  of web technologies, the complexity of web applications has also grown up. Specially making a web  application that works well with cross browser is a great challenge. Clearly, cross-browser means  something works with all versions of all browsers to have existed since the web began. By this paper we  have pointed out some reasons why applications behave or appear differently in different browsers because  if you know the cause, you can get a solution.
